Emergency

Fracture

Symptoms: Pain, swelling, deformity.
Treatment: Immobilize the limb, apply ice, seek immediate medical attention.

Bleeding

Symptoms: Blood flow from wound, shock symptoms.
Treatment: Apply pressure to wound, elevate limb if possible, seek emergency help.

Dehydration

Symptoms: Dry mouth, dizziness, decreased urine.
Treatment: Oral rehydration solutions, rest, seek medical advice if severe.

Severe Allergic Reaction

Symptoms: Swelling, difficulty breathing, rash.
Treatment: Use epinephrine injector if available, call emergency services.
